About Grow Up My Tree

Inspired by the concept of tree planting for carbon footprint reduction, our service lets you choose and plant fruit or timber trees. You help reduce your carbon footprint and enjoy long-term returns through fresh fruits or our buyback service.

Tree Plantation

Organic & Eco-friendly

Our mission to plant 1 billion fruit trees by 2030 in Uttar Pradesh. By Combining AI with (U.H.D.P) ultra-high density plantation method with organic practices which significantly boast the state’s economy and G.D.P. Our mission is made possible through crowd funding, allowing individuals and corporate to invest in sustainable agriculture and reach the reward of high-quality fruit.

HOW WE WORK

CHOOSE & BOOK

  • Fruit or white sandalwood
  • One-time booking: ₹25,000
  • Digital certificate & tree ID
  • Gift a tree option
  • Secure registration in our records

AI POWERED MONITORING

  • AI system tracks soil moisture, temperature & crop health
  • Sensors & drones for precision farming
  • Automated irrigation to avoid over/under watering
  • Predictive analytics for pests/diseases & yield forecasting
  • Dashboard alerts with regular photo/video updates

PLANT & SUSTAIN

  • UHDP orchards (1000–1200 trees/acre)
  • Organic compost & natural pest control
  • Annual fruits (e.g., 50% Of Production/yr*) or assured buyback
  • Transparent impact & growth reports
  • Long-term sustainability for 15 years

10 Reasons to Gift a Tree

Why tree gifting is the most meaningful, sustainable present you can give.

Zero Waste Gift

Your sustainable tree gift does not produce waste. Support an environmental project with a zero waste present.

Helps the Climate

Trees filter CO₂ from the air, helping fight global warming and making your gift a lasting gesture for the planet.

Meaningful Gift

Tree gifting is sensible and thoughtful, making people happy with a present that has deeper meaning.

Personal & Connecting

Planting a tree stands for bond, love, and loyalty. Personalize your gift for an unforgettable experience.

Investment in the Future

Each tree lives for years, creating a piece of the future and lasting impact for generations.

Natural Reserve

Trees prevent erosion, flooding, and help maintain natural reserves, making your gift valuable for nature.

Brings Joy

A tree gift is useful and beautiful, bringing joy to the recipient and everyone who enjoys nature.

Supports Biodiversity

Tree gifts create new living space for animals and plants, supporting biological diversity.

Creates Jobs

Tree gifting supports local jobs and communities, empowering farmers and villagers in reforestation areas.

Gift for All & the Planet

Your tree gift helps stop climate change and inspires others to live sustainably.
